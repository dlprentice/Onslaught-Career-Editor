# Start Here

Welcome to the **Onslaught Lore Book** — a curated reading order for the Battle Engine Aquila archive.

This edition is organized for reading, not raw file navigation. The curated experience is centered on `lore-book/`, and the **[table of contents](BOOK.md)** emphasizes flow and narrative while still linking to a small set of release-facing companion docs when they matter for the app.

Companion docs:
- [Roadmap Guide](roadmap/ROADMAP-INDEX.md)
- [Reverse Engineering Guide](reverse-engineering/RE-INDEX.md)
- [Project Overview](../README.MD)

## How to read this book

- Use the left-hand tree to move through chapters.
- Back/Forward/Home let you retrace your steps or return to the overview.
- Internal links stay inside the app.
- External links open your browser.

- **Lore & World**: story, factions, characters, and in-universe tech.
