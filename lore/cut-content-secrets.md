# Cut Content & Easter Eggs

## Cut Content & What-Ifs

### Confirmed Cut Features

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **3rd person camera** | For mech, was planned but cut |
| **More vegetation and creatures** | Planned for terrain |
| **Troop transport vehicles** | Early concept had player supporting troops more directly |
| **Full storyline** | Cut scenes were "cut up and sliced and reduced" |
| **Scrapped missions** | Likely existed that followed cut scenes |

### Community Discoveries

**Unreleased Content in Goodies** (discovered by community):
- **Baworo** found concept art of unreleased unit (Jan 2022): "I wonder why it was never added"
- **Jeppi** found spaceship-like concept art (Feb 2022) — speculation about planned space expansion
- Community speculation: sequel may have been planned with space missions

### Mysteries in the Released Game

**The Sea Monster**: A massive purple/pink spider-like creature emerges from the ocean during the submarine mission, does massive damage, then disappears forever. Never explained or seen again. Community wished satellite/submarine missions affected the ending.

**Muspell Homeland**: Players never visit the actual Muspell homeland — only see islands close by.

**Island of Saracen**: Appears on the game map but unclear if accessible in-game.

### Black & White Reference

According to Edge magazine, during development:
> "Lost Toys were tempted to have it feature an apple with a worm" — a reference to Black & White demos by their fellow Bullfrog alumni at Lionhead.

## Easter Eggs & Secrets

### Team Faces in Debriefing Screen

From Stuart (Discord #media, August 26, 2022):

> "Did you know that in the debriefing screen there was an Easter egg where tapping left and right back trigger1 together (on a pad) causes some face to appear instead of a goodie. The faces are from the team..."

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | Debriefing screen (after completing a level) |
| **Activation** | Tap left + right + back trigger1 together on controller |
| **Content** | Random team member faces appear instead of goodies |
| **Confirmed working** | PS2 emulator version |
| **PC version** | Stuart couldn't get it to work on PC |

### Gill-M Boss Named After Stuart

From Stuart (Discord #media, June 21, 2022):

> "The Gill-m was named after me... wasn't my choice."

The underwater boss "Gill-M" (and related `GillM.cpp`, `GillMHead.cpp` source files) is named after **Stuart Gillam** (Gill-M = Gillam). Stuart also coded this boss.

### The Lost Toys Football Team

The dev team had a football team where everyone's shirts were named after hobbit characters. Stuart's was **"Baggins"** — he still has the shirt somewhere.
