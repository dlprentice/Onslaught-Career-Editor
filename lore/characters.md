**Background**:
> Born in Forseti Minor in 1174, Hawk spent his youth on the wrong side of the tracks. Never much for formal education, he learnt his trade on the streets eventually finding his niche working at the docks. A natural aptitude for all things mechanical meant that he was assigned to storage duty, driving a load-lifter and moving cargo containers to where they needed to be.

> He gets his kicks from illicit **loader races** that take place in the docklands at night. In fact he's making quite a name for himself with his blend of aggression and raw piloting ability.

**Background**:
> Born in the Sohra Federation, Tatiana became a leading engineer and designer for the **Canseven Corporation**. Specialising in military applications, Canseven was renowned the world over for its advanced designs and foresight. Assigned to the Forseti Minor research centre, Tatiana moved over in 1190. Here she was able to satisfy her fascination with machines of all descriptions when a colleague took her along to a loader race down at the docks.

> A perfectionist, Tatiana expects the same of those around her. She does not have time for incompetence or excuses. Only the best will do.

> There is nothing that she does not know about Aquila. Her watchful and critical gaze has overseen every last system design and she is very protective about the whole thing.

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Born** | 1152 |

| **Height** | 5'11" |
| **Weight** | 182 lbs |
| **Hair** | Grey |
| **Eyes** | Brown |
| **Nationality** | Forseti |
| **Occupation** | Armed Forces, Commander of "Wild Cards" special forces unit |

**Background**:
> The son of **General Piet Kramer**, Chuck was groomed from the outset for the military life. His education was at **Forseti Military College** and he was inducted into the Armed Forces at the age of 16. Rather than follow his father all the way into the Army, Chuck elected to try out the Air Force.

> He distinguished himself in the **Tarn Insurrection of '72** when the peacekeepers escorting a convoy of refugees came under fire. Kramer and his wingman managed to destroy almost all of the attackers and drive the rest away with the loss of only two escort tanks. They were both awarded the **Air Cross** for saving the lives of the refugees.

> During the build up to the invasion, Kramer was assigned to the Battle Engine program as the military advisor. Following the start of hostilities, he returned with the new war machine to his special forces unit, the **'Wild Cards'**. He is a stern yet fair individual who values the lives of the people in his care even more than his own. He is determined to see this war out not only with every member of the Wild Cards still intact but distinguished with honour.

**Background**:
> Born on Castellian of **Muspellian immigrants**, Carver managed to create a whole new life for himself. He was able to construct a new background and join the military. Enrolling in the Airforce, he soon proved to be a top-notch fighter pilot. Naturally, he was eventually selected to become a test pilot for the latest projects. He helped develop the **F-24 Valkyrie Air Fighter** before being assigned to the Battle Engine program.

> Carver is an intelligent and calculating man. He has a **deep-rooted hatred of the Forseti** because of the way they treated his parents but he managed to keep all of this secret so that he would be ideally placed to really alter the course of the war. It didn't go entirely to plan as he wasn't able to make good his escape with the prototype, but the deaths of the entire test crew should set back the project for quite some time.
